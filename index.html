About me

When I was 12 my mother saw that I was really good with computers and enrolled me into Telerik Academy Algorithmic programming course with C++. It wasn't my thing but it showed me that I had skills to become a programmer. 3 years after that i decided to make my own Unity 3D videogame - Playground Racing (up on the Playstore). It helped me understand how to make visually pleasing UI and UX and how to write understandable code. After the game flopped (I had no marketing) I decided to make another game (Subway surfers - style) called Slice Run. Both projects took me over a year but i was committed to them and I finished them. But what I saw while creating them was that I enjoyed a lot more the front-end side of things. That's when I decided that I should become a front-end developer.
